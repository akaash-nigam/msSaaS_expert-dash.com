# Product Requirements Document: Expert Dash

## Overview
Expert Dash is a comprehensive dashboard platform for consultants and experts to manage their practice, client relationships, and knowledge sharing.

## Vision
Empower independent consultants and experts with professional tools to run their practice efficiently while building their reputation and growing their business.

## Target Audience
- Independent consultants
- Industry experts and advisors
- Freelance professionals
- Subject matter experts
- Executive coaches
- Fractional executives

## Core Features

### 1. Client Management
- Client portal for secure communication
- Project and engagement tracking
- Time tracking and billing
- Document sharing and collaboration
- Contract management

### 2. Knowledge Management
- Personal knowledge base
- Research and insights repository
- Best practices documentation
- Case study library
- AI-powered content organization

### 3. Scheduling & Booking
- Calendar integration
- Automated appointment booking
- Time zone management
- Video conferencing integration
- Reminders and notifications

### 4. Reputation Building
- Expert profile page
- Testimonial collection
- Content publishing platform
- Social proof integration
- SEO optimization

## Technical Stack
- Frontend: React + TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js + Express
- Database: PostgreSQL
- AI: GPT-4 for knowledge organization
- Integrations: Calendar (Google/Outlook), Video (Zoom/Teams), Payment (Stripe)

## Success Metrics
- Active consultants on platform
- Bookings per consultant
- Client satisfaction scores
- Revenue per consultant
- Platform retention rate

## Timeline
- Phase 1 (MVP): 4 months
- Phase 2 (Advanced features): 3 months
- Phase 3 (Marketplace): 3 months

## Competitive Advantage
- All-in-one solution for consultants
- AI-powered knowledge management
- Professional branding tools
- Integrated payment and scheduling
